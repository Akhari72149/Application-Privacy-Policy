# Privacy Policy for Akhari's Workbench

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Overview

Akhari's Workbench is designed to operate entirely on your device. We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your data remains under your control.

---

## Information We Collect

We do **not collect, store, or share any personal data**.

The app does not:

* Require user accounts
* Collect personal information
* Track user activity
* Use analytics services
* Use cookies or tracking technologies

---

## Local Data Storage

All data created or used within Akhari's Workbench is stored **locally on your device only**.

This may include:

* Tasks
* Notes
* Lists or board data

This data:

* Never leaves your device
* Is not transmitted to any server
* Is not accessible by us or any third party

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not share any data** with third parties.

---

## Internet Usage

Akhari's Workbench does **not require an internet connection** to function and does not transmit any data over the internet.

---

## Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device’s own security measures (such as passcodes or encryption).

---

## Children's Privacy

Akhari's Workbench does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or users of any age.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
